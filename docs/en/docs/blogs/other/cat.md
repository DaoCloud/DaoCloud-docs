## Products

- Meow Language Model: Collect cat vocalization data, recognize and convert to text, send to owner, help cat owners understand their cats
- Meow Face and Posture Model: Recognize cat faces and body postures like human face recognition, retrieve lost memories, find cat faces and postures that match human aesthetic standards, enable targeted recommendations
- Meow Language Translator: Output recognized text to various devices, allowing owners to understand cats
- Meow Chatbot: Convert human voice to cat sounds, enable conversation with cats, achieve mutual understanding between humans and cats
- Meow merchandise: clothing, toys, new products, second-hand items, etc.
